Bread

  • 5 lbs Flour, 1 bag
  • 2 envelopes Yeast
  • 2 tsp Sugar
  • 1 1/2 qt. Warm water more or less
  • 1/3 Cup Melted Crisco
  • 2 tbsp Salt

Put all ingredients in a large bowl except 2 or 3 cups flour.
Mix 1 qt warm water, yeast, crisco - add to flour and salt and mix thoroughly till you have a semi soft dough. Add more flour if needed or water to make your dough the right consistancy.
You can't hurt the bread by adding more water or more flour after you have used what the recipe calls for.
