Tomato Sauce - for Spaghetti

  • cut up spare ribs
  • chunks of beef
  • 1 large onion
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • mint
  • basil
  • nutmeg
  • dried celery
  • salt & pepper
  • large can of tomato paste

In a large pan, cook all ingredients in oil and marg. till meat is half done. Then add a large can of tomato paste.
Contadina preferred.
About 2 quts water - cook till meat is done - Keep adding water as it evaporates-
You should have about 2 qts sauce - Add more paste when neccessary.
Sauce should be fairly thick - when done.
